# Landing Page Structure

Route: `/`
Design: Clean, minimal, lots of whitespace

---

## 1. Navbar
- Logo: Jarumiri Studios
- Links: Services, Pricing, About
- CTA button: "Hire Me" → `/hire`

---

## 2. Hero
- Punchy headline (e.g. "Precision Editing. Your Vision, Delivered.")
- One-sentence subline about what you do
- Two CTAs:
  - "Hire Me" → `/hire`
  - "See My Work" → scrolls to reel section
- Background: subtle dark gradient or clean white — minimal

---

## 3. Reel / Work Samples
- Showreel embedded via YouTube or Vimeo (no self-hosting)
- Or a grid of 3–4 project thumbnails with short labels

---

## 4. Services
- 3–4 cards with icon, name, one-line description
- Examples:
  - Video Editing
  - Color Grading
  - Sound Design
  - Motion Graphics

---

## 5. Pricing
- 2–3 tiers (e.g. Basic / Standard / Premium)
- Each tier: price + bullet list of inclusions + "Get Started" button → `/hire`

---

## 6. About
- Short paragraph — background with DaVinci Resolve, editing style
- Optional photo

---

## 7. Footer
- Navigation links
- Telegram handle
- Copyright — Jarumiri Studios
